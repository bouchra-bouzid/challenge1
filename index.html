Traditional Italian Lasagna

✨ Traditional Dish

Savour this hearty homemade lasagna layered with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. Baked to golden perfection, it's the ultimate comfort food for any occasion.

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 500g ground beef
  • 400g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • A handful of fresh basil leaves (optional)

For the Béchamel Sauce:

  • 50g butter
  • 50g all-purpose flour
  • 500ml milk
  • A pinch of nutmeg (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For Assembly:

  • 9–12 dry lasagna sheets
  • 150g grated mozzarella cheese
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • Butter for greasing the baking dish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the dry lasagna sheets for 8–10 minutes until al dente. Drain and lay them flat on a clean towel to prevent sticking.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan, sauté the chopped onion and minced garlic until translucent.
  4. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  5. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15–20 minutes.
  6. Meanwhile, prepare the béchamel sauce: melt butter, whisk in flour to form a roux, then slowly add milk while stirring until smooth and thickened. Season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg.
  7. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce, then cover with lasagna sheets.
  8. Add a layer of meat sauce, then béchamel sauce, and sprinkle some mozzarella.
  9. Repeat the layers until ingredients are used, finishing with béchamel and a generous sprinkle of Parmesan cheese on top.
  10. Bake in the preheated oven for about 30 minutes, until golden and bubbly.
  11. Let rest 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
